Dear Deputy Chief Sylvester Ge:
On behalf of the Western Buddhist Association, we are honored to invite you to join us for the second New York Buddhist Cultural Festival, A Mindful Walk for Wellness, on Saturday, May 16, 2026, from 9:00 AM to 12:00 PM, beginning at Cadman Plaza Park and continuing across the Brooklyn Bridge.
Founded in 1998, the Western Buddhist Association established Fanyin Temple, recognized as the first Buddhist temple in Brooklyn. We organized the first New York Buddhist Cultural Festival last year and welcomed over 1,000 participants. This year's public gathering is expected to welcome thousands of participants, with the support and participation of Autism Speaks, LiveOn NY, Project Ready and CaringKind.
By bringing people together in one of New York City's most iconic public spaces, the event carries a simple but important message: when people cultivate a calmer and clearer mind, they are better able to care for others and turn compassion into action. In that spirit, for each completed walk, a $1 donation will be made to support individuals with autism and their families.
We would be deeply honored by your presence. We would also be grateful to invite you to offer brief remarks to the participants between 9:40 AM and 10:10 AM. We kindly ask that remarks remain nonpartisan and focused on community wellness. Afterward, we will begin the mindful walk across the Brooklyn Bridge.
If you are unable to attend personally, we would also be honored to welcome a representative from your office.
Thank you for your time and consideration.
With respectful regards,
Venerable Chang Kuan
Abbot and President, Western Buddhist Association
